Pork Chops

Picture of pork chops on tray

Basic information:

Prep Time:

10 mins

Cook Time:

10 mins

Additional Time

None

Total Time

20 mins

Servings

4

Fun facts

Pork chops are the champs of weeknight cooking. We've gathered the best ways to cook pork chops so they stay tender, moist, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon seasoning salt, or to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 (3/4 inch) thick pork chops

Steps

  1. Step

    2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Step

    3. Fry chops in hot oil until golden on the outside and juices run clear, 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
